Chicken Curry Recipes

Tito's Chicken Curry Recipes

Ingeridients

  • 1 cup chicken breasts
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Coriander, cumin, other spices of choice
  • Fresh onion and garlic
  • Soy sauce and Tomato paste
  • Cornstarch
  • Heavy cream
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Cut chicken breasts in half lengthwise slices.
  3. Cut chicken broth in half lengthwise slices.
  4. Add chicken broth to chicken breasts.
  5. Fry the onions in a deep pan with olive oil
  6. Fry the garlic in a deep pan with olive oil
  7. Add tomato paste and other spices to the fried onions and Garlic
  8. Add the chicken and a quater cup of the chicken broth
  9. Let it simmer for 20 minutes
  10. Add the Heavy cream and let it cook for 30 minutes
  11. Serve with preffered accompaniment
